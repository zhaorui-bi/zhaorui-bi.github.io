About

Research profile

Current focus

My current work centers on building machine learning methods that can support biological discovery. I am particularly interested in how foundation models and representation learning can be adapted to genomics, microbiome analysis, and protein science.

Background

I have worked across computer science, robotics, metagenomics, spatial omics, and biomolecular machine learning. This interdisciplinary path continues to shape how I approach both research questions and tool building.
